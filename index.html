Suggestions for a strong password:

1. Length :It should be atleast 8 characters long.
2. Complexity : Include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters,numbers and special characters.
2. Avoid personal information : Don't use name,username,phone number or birthday in your password.
4. Unique : Use a different password for each of your accounts to minimise the risk of multple accounts being compromised.
